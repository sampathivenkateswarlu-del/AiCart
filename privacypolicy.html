Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Introduction

At AiCart ("Company," "we," "us," or "our"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website www.aicart.com and use our services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

1. Information We Collect

A. Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, password, and profile information
  • Billing Information: Credit card numbers, billing address, and payment history
  • Shipping Information: Delivery address, shipping preferences
  • Communications: Messages, reviews, feedback, and customer service inquiries

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage Information: Pages visited, product viewed, search queries, time spent on pages
  • Cookies & Tracking: Browsing history, preferences, login information
  • Location Information: Approximate location based on IP address

C. Information from Third Parties

  • Payment processors and financial institutions
  • Shipping and delivery partners
  • Social media platforms (if you link your account)
  • Marketing and analytics partners

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • Processing and fulfilling your orders
  • Providing customer support and responding to inquiries
  • Improving our website and services
  • Personalizing your shopping experience with AI recommendations
  • Sending marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Preventing fraud and ensuring security
  • Complying with legal and regulatory obligations
  • Conducting research and analytics

3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf (payment processors, shipping partners)
  • Business Partners: Selected partners for joint offerings
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transitions: In case of merger, acquisition, or bankruptcy

We do NOT: Sell or rent your personal information to marketers or third parties for their own marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Encrypted storage for sensitive data
  • Secure servers with firewalls and intrusion detection
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Employee access controls and confidentiality agreements
  • PCI DSS compliance for payment card data

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

5. Your Rights and Choices

A. Access and Correction

You have the right to access, update, or correct your personal information at any time through your account or by contacting us.

B. Opt-Out of Marketing

You can opt out of promotional emails by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link in marketing emails or adjusting your preferences in your account settings.

C. Cookie Management

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

D. GDPR Rights (EU Residents)

If you are a resident of the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Object to processing

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Typically:

  • Account information is retained while your account is active
  • Transaction data is retained for 7 years for tax and legal purposes
  • Marketing data is retained until you opt out
  • Cookies are retained as per their expiration date

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies before providing your information.

8. Children's Privacy

AiCart services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected information from a child without parental consent, we will delete it immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on our website and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of these changes.
