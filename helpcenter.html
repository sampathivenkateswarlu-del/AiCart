Frequently Asked Questions

What is AiCart?

AiCart is an AI-powered online shopping marketplace that uses artificial intelligence to provide personalized product recommendations, competitive prices, and exceptional customer service.

Is my personal information safe on AiCart?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your personal and payment information. Our security practices comply with international standards.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days for a full refund, no questions asked.

How long does shipping usually take?

Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping is available for 2-3 business days. Delivery times may vary based on your location and product availability.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to 45+ countries worldwide. International shipping fees and estimated delivery times vary by destination. You can check shipping details during checkout.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our 24/7 customer support team via email, phone, live chat, or social media. Support is available in 25 languages.
