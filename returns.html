Returns & Refunds

Hassle-Free Returns Within 30 Days

Our Return Policy

At AiCart, your satisfaction is our priority. We offer a straightforward 30-day return policy with no questions asked.

✓ 30-Day Return Window

Return items within 30 days of purchase for a full refund or exchange.

Return Conditions

To be eligible for return, items must meet the following conditions:

  • Items must be returned within 30 days of delivery
  • Products must be unused and in original condition
  • Original packaging and tags must be intact
  • All accessories and documentation must be included
  • Items must not show signs of wear or damage

Note: Certain items like perishables, electronics without original packaging, or customized products may have different return policies.

How to Return an Item

Step 1: Initiate the Return

Log into your AiCart account and navigate to "My Orders." Select the item you wish to return and click "Return Item."

Step 2: Choose Return Reason

Select the reason for your return from the available options. This helps us improve our service.

Step 3: Get Return Label

We'll provide you with a prepaid return shipping label. Print it and attach it to your package.

Step 4: Ship It Back

Drop off your package at any participating shipping location. You can track the return shipment online.

Step 5: Receive Refund

Once we receive and inspect your return, your refund will be processed within 5-7 business days.

Refund Processing

Refund Timeline

Initial Review: 1-2 business days after we receive your return

Processing: 5-7 business days after approval

Bank Transfer: 5-10 business days (depending on your bank)

Refund Methods

Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for the purchase. If you paid with:

  • Credit/Debit Card: Refund appears on your statement within 10 business days
  • Digital Wallet: Refund processed within 3-5 business days
  • Bank Transfer: Refund appears within 10 business days

Exchanges

If you'd like to exchange an item for a different size, color, or product instead of getting a refund, we can arrange that for you.

Simply note your exchange preference when initiating the return, and we'll process the exchange with minimal fees.

Special Circumstances

Damaged or Defective Items

If you received a damaged or defective item, you can request a replacement or refund without worrying about the condition of the product. Simply contact us with photos of the damage.

Wrong Item Received

If we sent you the wrong item, we'll take it back and send the correct one at no extra cost. Refunds are issued immediately upon return.

Item Not as Described

If the item doesn't match the product description on our website, you can return it for a full refund or replacement.
