Delivery Information

Delivery Address

We ship to residential and commercial addresses. Please provide a complete address during checkout. Parcels are delivered to your address as specified in the shipping label.

Delivery Times

Estimated delivery times are calculated from the order placement date. Delivery times may vary due to weather, holidays, or carrier exceptions. Orders placed on weekends or holidays are processed the next business day.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, we'll send you an email with a tracking number. You can track your package in real-time on our website and the carrier's website.

International Shipping

We ship internationally to over 45 countries. International orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes.

Customs & Duties

International customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or brokerage fees. These are determined by the destination country and are not included in our shipping costs.

Documentation

We provide all necessary customs documentation. Our team will help if any additional information is required by customs authorities.

Restricted Items

Some items cannot be shipped internationally due to regulations. Check the product page for international shipping availability.

Shipping Protection

All AiCart shipments include basic protection. Optional shipping protection is available for high-value items.

  • Basic Protection: Included free with all orders
  • Insurance: Optional insurance available for items over $500
  • Signature Required: Available for high-value orders
  • Fragile Handling: Special packaging for delicate items

Shipping Issues

Delayed Delivery

If your order is delayed beyond the estimated delivery window, please contact us. We'll investigate with the carrier and may offer compensation.

Lost Packages

If your package is marked as delivered but you haven't received it, contact us immediately. We'll file a claim with the carrier.

Damaged Shipments

If your package arrives damaged, document the damage with photos and contact us within 48 hours of delivery.
